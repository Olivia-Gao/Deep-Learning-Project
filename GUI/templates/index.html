Solar Cell Image Dataset

The Solar Cell Defect Dataset contains high-resolution images of solar cells, labeled with different types of defects such as cracks, dirt, and other anomalies. This dataset is useful for training and testing deep learning models to automatically detect defects in solar cells and enhance their manufacturing processes.
